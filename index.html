New to Valorant?

Follow this simple path to go from confused to clutching rounds with your friends.

  1. Step 1

    Pick one main agent

    Start with a single agent and learn that kit deeply before swapping every game.

  2. Step 2

    Learn a default on each map

    Know where you like to play on attack and defense so you aren’t guessing every round.

  3. Step 3

    Master two rifles

    Get comfortable with Phantom or Vandal plus one sidearm for most eco rounds.

Game modes

Switch up how you learn the game – from sweaty ranked to quick spike rush games.

Unrated

Best place to warm up new agents, guns, and lineups with less pressure.

  • Standard 5v5, first to 13 rounds.
  • Perfect for learning maps and basic comms.

Competitive

Play your main comp and take economy, utility, and teamplay seriously.

  • Ranked ladder with RR and MMR.
  • Track your progress season to season.

Spike Rush

Fast games with random guns and orbs to practice aim and abilities.

  • Best of 7 rounds, quick queue times.
  • Great for warming up before ranked.

Custom games

Perfect for lineups, 1v1s, and practicing site executes with friends.

  • No RR, fully sandboxed lobbies.
  • Experiment with setups without losing rank.
